What we believe

The EMG Standard.

The principles behind how we choose creators, evaluate brand opportunities, build relationships, and make long-term decisions.

We do not sign creators simply because they have the biggest following.

We look for trust, talent, consistency, professionalism, a distinct voice, and the ability to build something meaningful.

We do not chase every campaign.

We pursue partnerships where the creator, brand, audience, message, and expectations can align honestly.

We do not believe creators should depend entirely on platforms they do not control.

Creator Hubs provide another place to showcase, collaborate, grow, and build lasting value.

We do not believe data replaces relationships.

Technology and insight should help people make better decisions—not remove the human judgment that makes partnerships work.

We do not promise what we cannot guarantee.

No agency can guarantee deals, growth, income, reach, or campaign outcomes. We communicate honestly and work intentionally.

We do not build only for today.

We make decisions with the creator's career, the brand's reputation, the audience's trust, and the ecosystem's future in mind.

What we ask of creators

  • Be honest with your audience and with EMG
  • Meet commitments and communicate early
  • Protect the trust that made your platform valuable
  • Respect partners, collaborators, and communities
  • Stay open to learning, feedback, and growth

What brands can expect

  • Thoughtful creator recommendations
  • Clear scopes and realistic expectations
  • Professional communication and coordination
  • Respect for creator authenticity and brand objectives
  • A partner focused on long-term value, not just transaction volume

Shared standards create stronger partnerships.
