What we look for

Trust matters more than vanity metrics.

We are intentionally developing a selective, multi-category roster. Follower count can open a conversation, but it does not decide the outcome.

  • A clear voice and identifiable point of view
  • Genuine audience trust and engagement
  • Consistency and a professional work ethic
  • Respect for brand partners and community
  • A desire to build for the long term
  • Openness to collaboration and strategic guidance

Representation should be a fit on both sides.

We do not promise instant deals, guaranteed income, or overnight growth. We promise honest communication, intentional work, professional advocacy, and a model built around long-term opportunity.

Every application is reviewed. Because our roster is selective, applying does not guarantee representation.
