EMG Talent Group is the creator representation division of Elite Media Group LLC.

Why EMG exists

Creators gained audiences. Brands gained a powerful new channel. But the infrastructure supporting those relationships remained fragmented, manual, and overly focused on short-term transactions.

EMG was built to connect experienced representation with technology, owned media, business strategy, and a model that creates more ways for creators and brands to build lasting value.

Creators deserve more than management. Brands deserve more than spreadsheets.
