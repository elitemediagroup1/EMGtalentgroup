Privacy Policy

Effective July 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Elite Media Group LLC, operating EMG Talent Group (“EMG,” “we,” “us,” or “our”), collects, uses, and protects information submitted through emgtalentgroup.com.

Information we collect

We may collect contact information, company information, social profile links, creator application details, campaign requirements, budget ranges, website usage information, and any other information you voluntarily submit.

How we use information

We use information to review creator applications, respond to inquiries, evaluate brand and talent fit, develop business relationships, administer the website, prevent abuse, improve services, and comply with legal obligations.

Forms and service providers

Website forms may be processed and stored by Netlify and routed to hello@elitemediagroup.io. Hosting, analytics, email, security, and other service providers may process data on our behalf under their own terms and privacy practices.

Public information

When a creator submits public social profile links, EMG may review information publicly available through those profiles for evaluation and business purposes.

Cookies and analytics

The site may use essential technologies and, if enabled, analytics tools to understand aggregate usage. Additional notices or consent controls may be implemented where required.

Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We may share information with service providers, professional advisors, business partners when necessary to evaluate or administer a requested opportunity, and authorities when required by law.

Retention and security

We retain information for as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described. No system is completely secure, but we use reasonable administrative and technical measures appropriate to the information involved.

Your choices

You may request access, correction, or deletion of personal information by emailing hello@elitemediagroup.io. We may need to verify the request and retain information where legally permitted or required.

Children

This website is not intended for children under 13. Creator applicants who are minors must involve a parent or legal guardian before entering any representation relationship.

Changes

We may update this policy as our services evolve. The effective date above identifies the latest version.
