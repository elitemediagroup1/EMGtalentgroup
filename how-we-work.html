Clear from the beginning

A process built for better partnerships.

Creators and brands need different paths, but both deserve clear expectations, professional communication, and thoughtful execution.

For creators

01 — Discover

We learn your story, audience, work, goals, strengths, and the business behind your content.

02 — Evaluate

We assess mutual fit, category position, professionalism, opportunities, and support needs.

03 — Build

We establish positioning, materials, systems, communication, and the foundation for representation.

04 — Pursue

We identify and develop opportunities aligned with your audience, credibility, and long-term goals.

05 — Execute

We support negotiation, contracting, deliverables, timelines, and partner communication.

06 — Compound

We use each project, relationship, Creator Hub contribution, and insight to inform what comes next.

For brands

01 — Define

Clarify the goal, audience, message, platforms, timeline, geography, deliverables, and budget.

02 — Curate

Evaluate creators for fit, credibility, content style, availability, and campaign role.

03 — Align

Confirm scope, usage, compensation, creative expectations, approvals, and success criteria.

04 — Coordinate

Manage contracts, communication, deadlines, assets, reviews, and stakeholder expectations.

05 — Launch

Support execution while protecting authentic creator voice and the agreed brand direction.

06 — Learn

Review outputs, feedback, performance signals, and next-step opportunities.

Bring us the objective. We will help shape the path.
